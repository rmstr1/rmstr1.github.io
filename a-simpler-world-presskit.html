Description

A Simpler World is a small, cozy building game that focuses on creativity and relaxation. Start on an overgrown island, clean it up, and turn it into your own little home. All decorations are available from the start, so you're free to decorate both indoors and outdoors however you like.

There are no objectives, but if you've always wanted to relax in a charming pixel art world, this might the game for you!

Also, there's pets!

Features

  • Randomly generated islands: Choose from a variety of randomly generated islands to build on.
  • Decorate freely: Access every prop right away, no need to unlock anything.
  • Relaxed play: No goals, no pressure, just chill and create something pretty.
  • Pets: Pets!
