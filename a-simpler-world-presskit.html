Description

A Simpler World is a cozy sandbox game where you create your own charming pixel-art diorama. Begin with a procedurally generated island, place your home, and gradually bring your world to life. With every prop unlocked from the very start, you’re able to decorate both the landscape and your interiors freely, crafting a tranquil environment to the full extent of your imagination.

Features

  • Discover Unique Islands - Start fresh on a variety of randomly generated islands, each offering a different canvas for your creativity.
  • Decorate Freely - Access the full library of props right away to personalize your surroundings and interior spaces.
  • Set the Atmosphere - Adjust weather and lighting to create the perfect mood for your world, from sunny afternoons to cozy twilight.

About Apes of the Screen

Apes of the Screen is a one-person independent game development studio based in Graz, Austria. Specializing in pixel art games, I use generative art and pixel aesthetics to create immersive virtual worlds. In addition to game development, I build dedicated tools for asset creation to streamline workflows and expand creative possibilities.
