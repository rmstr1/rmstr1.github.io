About

[[Normal Bytes Studio]] is a one-person independent game development studio based in Graz, Austria. Specializing in indie pixel art games, I use generative art and pixel aesthetics to create immersive virtual worlds. In addition to game development, I build dedicated tools for asset creation to streamline workflows and expand creative possibilities.
